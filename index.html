About Me

         
 

I'm Kaitlyn, a NJ native and third-year at UC Berkeley (class of 2022), double majoring in applied math and data science with concentrations in operations research and business analytics.

 

I'm very passionate about early STEM education and using tech for social good. Some of my favorite things I'm involved in on campus are the Berkeley Math Tournament, research for the Graduate School of Education, and volunteer coaching elementary math olympiad teams.

 

Outside of school, I love spending my free time eating mac & cheese, trying new boba shops, hiking, photography, and playing softball. A few things on my bucketlist before graduating are to visit Yosemite, Lake Tahoe, Zion, paraglide, and to join the archery club. See more on the Berkeley blog.
