about this website

permanent under construction notice

Just like myself, this website will always be evolving as I learn more about webdesign and document my adventures through my few remaining semesters at school. I took an introductory webdesign decal at the recommendation of one of my housemates, and it ended up being one of my favorite courses. This website was created using the knowledge I've learned from the class in addition to what I've self-studied over the summer. While somewhat simple, it was a very fun learning journey. However, not all the art on the website is my own, and I'd like to credit the authors below.
